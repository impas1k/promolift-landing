Your Lash Lift Journey

Lash Lamination / Lift — FAQ

A lash lift is a professional treatment that uses a gentle perming solution to curl your natural lashes from the root, creating an upward lift that makes your eyes appear wider and more awake. The lashes are set on a silicone rod, treated with a lifting lotion and a setting lotion, and finished with a keratin-infused conditioner. The result is a beautiful, natural curl that lasts 6 to 8 weeks.
Yes! Lash extensions involve attaching synthetic fibres to each natural lash, while a lash lift works with your own lashes, curling them from the root for a natural, low-maintenance look. A lash lift requires no daily upkeep, no special sleeping positions, and causes no damage to your natural lashes. It is the perfect alternative for anyone wanting a natural enhancement without extensions.
Results typically last 6 to 8 weeks, depending on your natural lash growth cycle. As your lashes naturally shed and new ones grow in, the effect gradually fades. To maintain your lifted look, we recommend booking a repeat session every 6 to 8 weeks.
Yes, when performed by a trained professional. We use only professional-grade, keratin-infused products that are gentle on your lashes. The treatment actually conditions and strengthens your lashes. It is important to follow aftercare instructions: avoid water, steam, and mascara for the first 24 to 48 hours.
Absolutely! A lash tint is the perfect complement to a lash lift. The tint darkens your lashes, making them appear thicker and more defined, while the lift opens up your eyes. Together they create a dramatic, mascara-like effect without any daily effort. Ask about our lash lift and tint combo.

Your Brow Lamination Journey

Brow Lamination / Lift — FAQ

Brow lamination is a professional brow styling treatment that sets your brow hairs in an upward, brushed position. A gentle perming solution is applied to break down the hair bonds, then the brows are brushed into the desired shape and a setting solution locks them in place. The result is full, fluffy, perfectly styled brows that last 4 to 6 weeks.
Yes! Brow lamination is especially effective for sparse or unruly brows. The treatment lifts and repositions every hair, creating the illusion of fuller, thicker brows. When combined with tinting, it can dramatically transform thin or patchy brows into a full, defined shape without the need for microblading or daily filling in.
Results typically last 4 to 6 weeks, depending on your natural hair growth cycle. As new hairs grow in and the treated hairs naturally shed, the effect gradually fades. To maintain your lifted look, we recommend booking a repeat session every 4 to 6 weeks.
Absolutely! Brow tinting is available as an add-on to your lamination treatment. The tint fills in sparse areas, darkens light brow hairs, and creates a more defined look that complements the laminated, brushed-up effect. This combo is our most popular brow service — perfect for achieving Instagram-ready brows.
Keep your brows dry for the first 24 hours, avoid touching or brushing them excessively, and do not use skincare products or makeup directly on the brows. Avoid steam, saunas, and excessive sweating for the first 48 hours. After that, your brows can be gently brushed into place. Use a nourishing brow oil daily to keep hairs conditioned.
